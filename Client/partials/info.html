Information

This application is built with the intention to support the task of utility companies by making it easier to make data-based decisions regarding groundwater and by showing data on a geographical map.

Future efforts must be carefully measured with the expected impact and compared with current efforts.
A data-driven decision support tool should help prioritize future plans for the areas located upstairs and in the vicinity of drilling, recovery and recovery areas for the purpose of ensuring groundwater.

http://www.vandogaffald.dk/ This application have been developed by GeoPartner A/S in cooperation with Vand og Affald Svendborg.
